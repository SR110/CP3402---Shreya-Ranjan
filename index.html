About Us

We are a group of people motivated to greatly reduce the plastics found in the seas and largely reduce the effect on the sea creatures. We want to create a culture in which single-use plastics are eliminated to a great extent. To achieve this, we have set up vending machines at various institutions, MRT stations and malls across Singapore.
